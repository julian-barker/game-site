Fun Facts

History

  • Tetris was invented by Alexey Pajitnov in 1984



  • The idea for Tetris was from a game called "Pentonimos," which had 5 squared pieces



  • The Tetris music is a 19th century folk song called "Korobeiniki"



  • In 2012 Tetris was aquired by the Museum of Modern Art in NYC

Facts

  • Tetris is in the Guiness Book of World Records: Gamers Edition



  • Tetris was the first game in space on a Russian mission in 1993



  • Tetris can change the shape of your brain and contribute to greater cognitive efficiency



  • Tetris can cause hallucinations known as "Tetris Effect," gaming-induced psuedo-hallucinations, or game transfer phenomena
